Gamification

Gamification of robot control lowers the inhibition threshold for non-specialists and makes the field of robot programming/control accessible to a broader mass. This can be used for education for pupils, students or new workers to offer them an easy introduction to the subject. In addition, the hurdles of getting used to or learning how to program robots will be lower since it is a familiar input device.

Interfaces

The interaction between humans and robots is realized with the help of the gaming controller through an innovative user interface. In addition, feedback, one of the most important elements of human learning, has been integrated and will play a significant role in future human-robot collaboration. How can the robot operator be enabled to create sensitive and precise programs? To do this, he should sense what the robot senses (through its sensors) - and the robot operator can do this with this mock-up.
